What it is

STRONK logs your sets. Tracks your weight. Tells you how many honeybees you theoretically lifted today.

The math is real. The shopping cart full of bees is a creative choice.

STRONK does not have a coach. STRONK does not send motivational notifications. STRONK does not know your goals and — with respect — does not want to. You showed up. STRONK logged it. Everybody goes home.

Stronkness

The math is real. The units are not the point.

After you log a session, STRONK does a quick calculation and converts your total tonnage into something mathematically accurate and completely absurd. The delivery is flat. STRONK is not making a big deal of what you lifted. STRONK is making a big deal of the bees.
